What To Consider

A refinance is a strategy, not just a transaction

Before recommending anything, we weigh the full picture: any penalty to break your current term, the new rate and structure, closing costs, and how the move serves your longer-term plan.

  • Penalty vs. benefit analysis
  • Equity available and loan-to-value

  • Fixed vs. variable and term length
